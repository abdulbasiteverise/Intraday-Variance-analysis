Intraday Variance Analysis for Workforce Management

This dashboard helps Workforce Management analysts analyze intraday call arrival patterns, compare forecast vs actual volume, detect spikes, estimate end-of-day volume using run-rate and blended forecasting models, and assess backlog risk in real time.

It is designed for call center operations teams to monitor traffic behaviour, identify early warning signs of volume spikes, and adjust staffing strategies throughout the day.
